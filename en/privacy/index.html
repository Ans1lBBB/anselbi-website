1. Data Collection

Our apps work without account registration. We do not ask for your name, email, or phone number. We do not operate a member database and cannot identify who you are in real life.

To improve our apps, ProfitFairy and RubyDays use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage events (such as which screens you open or whether a calculation or diary save was completed). This data is used only to understand how the app is used and to improve the product. It cannot reveal your personal identity and does not include period dates, diary text, or photos.

In-app purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple. We do not receive your Apple ID or payment details. We only check on your device whether VIP features are unlocked.

2. Advertising & Third-Party Services

ProfitFairy and AIOS Sea integrate Google AdMob ads to remain free. If you allow app tracking, AdMob may use a device advertising identifier (such as IDFA) to deliver personalized ads and measure ad performance. We do not link that identifier to your name or account. You can turn this off anytime in iPhone or iPad Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. Ads may not appear in the EU, UK, Switzerland, and similar regions.

RubyDays and all iMessage sticker packs contain no advertisements, providing a completely ad-free experience.

Google (AdMob and Firebase Analytics) Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Local Storage & Privacy

Health data (RubyDays cycle and diary entries), locked photos, profit calculation history, game progress, and card data are stored only on your device (optional private iCloud sync) and are never uploaded to the developer's servers. Firebase Analytics receives only anonymous interaction events and never includes that health or diary content.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All sticker and app content is family-friendly.

5. Policy Changes & Contact

Any material changes to this privacy policy will be announced on this page. For privacy-related questions or data requests, please contact us using the button below.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. No registration is required to use any of our apps. To improve product quality, we collect anonymous crash reports and usage statistics via Apple's framework, which cannot identify individual users.

2. Advertising & Third-Party Services

ProfitFairy and AIOS Realm integrate Google AdMob ads to remain free. AdMob may collect anonymous advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA) as per its policy, but these are not linked to your identity. You can limit ad tracking in your device settings.

RubyDays and all iMessage sticker packs contain no advertisements, providing a completely ad-free experience.

Google AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Local Storage & Privacy

All health data (RubyDays), diary entries, locked photos, profit calculation history, game progress, and card data are stored only on your device and are never uploaded to any external server.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All sticker and app content is family-friendly.

Any material changes to this privacy policy will be announced on this page. For privacy-related questions or data requests, please contact us using the button below.
